Biryani

Description

Biryani is rich and aromatic Indian rice dish, made with fragrant basmati rice, tender meat, and blend of spices. It's known for its colourful layers and delicious taste, ofter garnished with fried onions and fresh herbs.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups basmati rice
  • 500g chicken (cut into pieces)
  • 2 onions, thinly sliced
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • 1/2 cup yogurt
  • 2 tsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • 4 cups water
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh coriander and mint leaves
  • 2 tbsp cooking oil or ghee

Steps

  1. Rinse the basmati rice and soak it in water for 20–30 minutes.
  2. Heat oil or ghee in a large pan and fry the sliced onions until golden brown. Remove half for garnishing.
  3. Add the ginger-garlic paste and sauté for a minute, then add tomatoes, yogurt, and spices (turmeric, chili powder, garam masala).
  4. Add chicken pieces and cook until they are well coated with the masala and slightly browned.
  5. In a separate pot, boil water with a little salt and cook the soaked rice until 70% done. Drain and set aside.
  6. Layer the half-cooked rice over the chicken mixture, sprinkle fried onions, coriander, and mint leaves on top.
  7. Cover the pan with a tight lid and cook on low heat (dum) for 20–25 minutes until the rice is fully cooked and fluffy.
  8. Gently fluff up the biryani and serve hot with raita or salad.
